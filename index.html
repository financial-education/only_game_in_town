1. Three Types of Traders

The table below lists the three types of traders that the market maker confronts. The second column indicates whether the market maker generally gains (+) or loses (-) from trading against each type. The third columns describes each trader type's motivation for trading.

Type MM +/-? Description
Informed - Has information on asset values the market doesn't
Noise + Thinks they have special information, but actually doesn't
Liquidity + Trades for typical investment purposes
